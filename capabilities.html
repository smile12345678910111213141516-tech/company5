Vertically Integrated

We Buy. We Rebuild. We Own.

Our Engine for Value

Because we purchase existing solar assets to hold in our own portfolio, our vertically integrated approach is built entirely on long-term value creation. From precise pre-acquisition diagnostics to comprehensive rebuilding and operations, we handle every step.

Acquisition Due Diligence

Before extending a purchase offer, our engineering team conducts exhaustive evaluations to ensure the asset can be successfully rebuilt for our portfolio.

Performance Analytics

Deep production analysis using PVsyst modeling and thermal imaging to identify uplift potential.

Component Forensics

Electroluminescence, IV curve tracing, and hotspot analysis to scope our rebuilding parameters.

Direct Financial Modeling

Internal cash flow projections optimizing the asset exclusively for our acquisition yield.

Post-Acquisition Stabilization

Immediate action taken on distressed assets we acquire:

  • Emergency O&M deployment
  • Clearing deferred maintenance
  • Compliance and safety remediation
  • Working capital injection

Portfolio Repowering

Comprehensive rebuilding to modernize our assets:

  • Utility-scale inverter replacement
  • Degraded module restoration
  • System architecture modernization
  • Structural and tracker enhancements

Principal Operations

Long-term management of our owned portfolio:

  • Predictive and preventative maintenance
  • Deep SCADA integrations
  • Yield maximization strategies
  • Continuous performance improvement

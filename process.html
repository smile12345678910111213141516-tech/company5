Methodology

Transaction-to-Operations Process

Five disciplined phases ensuring rigorous evaluation and measurable performance improvement.

Initial Screening

Rapid asset qualification based on vintage, technology, location, and distress indicators. We assess remaining useful life, performance ratios, and market context to prioritize opportunities.

Due Diligence

Integrated technical and financial review including on-site inspections, production data analysis, O&M record review, and evaluation of PPAs, leases, and interconnection agreements.

Transaction Execution

Tax-efficient acquisition structuring with legal, tax, and financing coordination. We minimize friction while protecting investor interests and ensuring operational continuity.

Repowering & Optimization

Prioritized improvement plan implementation: component replacements, protocol enhancements, and system optimizations with transparent stakeholder communication.

Asset Management

Steady-state operations with continuous monitoring, preventive maintenance, and strategic planning for future capital improvements and value creation.

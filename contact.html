Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What types of assets do you acquire?

We focus on utility-scale and commercial solar assets 1MW and larger, particularly those from 2010-2016 vintage facing performance issues, needing equipment replacement, or in financial distress.

How long does the evaluation process take?

Initial screening takes 48-72 hours. Full due diligence typically requires 2-4 weeks depending on asset complexity and information availability.

Is my information kept confidential?

Absolutely. All discussions begin with a mutual non-disclosure agreement. We treat all asset information with the highest level of confidentiality.

What if my asset has existing debt or tax equity?

We have extensive experience structuring transactions that preserve or transfer existing tax equity and work constructively with lenders on workout or consent scenarios.

Who We Are

Principal Buyers of Existing Solar Infrastructure

Sell Your Asset

Repowering Partners is a specialized acquisition company. Instead of developing greenfield projects or acting merely as consultants, we focus entirely on buying, rebuilding, and operating existing solar generation assets for our own long-term portfolio.

We provide a reliable, direct exit strategy for current owners of aging, underperforming, or distressed solar farms. Because we rebuild these assets for ourselves, our multidisciplinary team of engineers and financial models identifies deep value creation opportunities that conventional secondary buyers miss.

Our Principal Ownership Model

With massive fleets of early-generation solar approaching critical maintenance milestones, we are the buyers stepping into the breach. We acquire the assets directly, utilize our capital and operational expertise to extend facility lifespans 15-25 years, and boost energy yield by bringing them onto our own optimized platform.

Methodology

Transaction-to-Operations Process

Five disciplined phases ensuring rigorous evaluation and measurable performance improvement for every solar asset we acquire.

Phase 1: Initial Screening

Rapid asset qualification based on vintage, technology, location, and distress indicators. We assess remaining useful life, performance ratios, and market context to prioritize opportunities.

  • Technology stack evaluation (modules, inverters, racking)
  • Location and interconnection analysis
  • PPA/lease terms review
  • Initial performance ratio assessment
  • Timeline: 48-72 hours

Phase 2: Due Diligence

Integrated technical and financial review including on-site inspections, production data analysis, O&M record review, and evaluation of PPAs, leases, and interconnection agreements.

  • 12-24 month production data analysis
  • On-site equipment inspection
  • Electroluminescence and thermal imaging
  • IV curve tracing and hotspot analysis
  • Environmental and permit compliance review
  • Timeline: 2-4 weeks

Phase 3: Transaction Execution

Tax-efficient acquisition structuring with legal, tax, and financing coordination. We minimize friction while protecting investor interests and ensuring operational continuity.

  • Asset purchase agreement negotiation
  • Tax equity structuring and preservation
  • Lender coordination and consent
  • Regulatory filings and approvals
  • Timeline: 4-8 weeks

Phase 4: Repowering & Optimization

Prioritized improvement plan implementation: component replacements, protocol enhancements, and system optimizations with transparent stakeholder communication.

  • Emergency stabilization (if needed)
  • Inverter replacement or repair
  • Module cleaning and restoration
  • SCADA system upgrades
  • Vegetation management
  • Timeline: 90-180 days

Phase 5: Asset Management

Steady-state operations with continuous monitoring, preventive maintenance, and strategic planning for future capital improvements and value creation.

  • 24/7 monitoring and alerts
  • Preventive maintenance scheduling
  • Monthly performance reporting
  • Quarterly investor updates
  • Annual improvement planning
  • Timeline: Ongoing

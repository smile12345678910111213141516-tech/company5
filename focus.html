Portfolio Ownership

Target Acquisition Profile

We are actively buying solar farms across the U.S. Because we rebuild and operate these assets in our own portfolio, we specialize in directly acquiring complex, aging, and underperforming systems that other buyers avoid.

Distressed Assets

We provide direct exits for owners and lenders of challenged facilities:

  • Lender workouts & receivership
  • Sponsor / tax equity exits
  • High deferred maintenance
  • Immediate capital injections required

Aging Infrastructure

We specifically purchase 2010-2016 vintage projects to rebuild them:

  • Obsolete inverter replacements
  • Degraded module swap-outs
  • Ailing tracker infrastructure
  • Expired warranty systems

Underperformance

We acquire assets yielding below baseline and solve the issues ourselves:

  • Severe clipping & shading
  • Outdated SCADA integrations
  • Soiling footprint constraints
  • Poor original engineering

Repowering Plays

We look for projects ripe for our engineering upgrades:

  • AC-side inverter retrofits
  • DC-side expansion feasibility
  • Bifacial module integration
  • Energy storage addition plays

Scale & Scope

We purchase facilities 1MW and larger, including:

  • Utility-scale PV generation
  • Community solar farms
  • Large C&I distributed systems
  • Portfolio acquisitions

Key Geographies

We are expanding our ownership footprint in U.S. growth corridors:

  • California & Southwest
  • Texas & ERCOT lines
  • Florida & Southeast
  • Midwest solar networks

Methodology

Transaction-to-Operations Process

Five disciplined phases ensuring rigorous evaluation and measurable performance improvement.

Initial Screening

Rapid asset qualification based on vintage, technology, location, and distress indicators. We assess remaining useful life to prioritize opportunities.

Due Diligence

Integrated technical and financial review including on-site inspections, production data analysis, and evaluation of PPAs and interconnection agreements.

Transaction Execution

Tax-efficient acquisition structuring with legal, tax, and financing coordination. We minimize friction while protecting investor interests.

Repowering & Optimization

Prioritized improvement plan implementation: component replacements, protocol enhancements, and system optimizations.

Asset Management

Steady-state operations with continuous monitoring, preventive maintenance, and strategic planning for value creation.

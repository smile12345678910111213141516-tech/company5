Methodology

Our Acquisition & Repowering Process

How we buy, rebuild, and operate existing assets. A streamlined path from initial valuation to our long-term ownership.

Acquisition Screening & Valuation

We perform rapid diagnostic qualification on distressed or aging solar assets based on market context, vintage, and location. Because we buy explicitly to repower for ourselves, we move quickly to identify if an asset fits our portfolio.

Deep Due Diligence

Our integrated team steps in for a rigorous technical and financial review. Since we take principal ownership, our inspections, physical testing, and contract evaluations are solely to map out our future rebuilding costs and timeline.

Direct Purchase & Execution

We negotiate direct, tax-efficient acquisitions with current owners or lenders. Our goal is a swift close that provides the current operators their necessary exit while we stabilize the asset for our control.

Comprehensive Repowering

Once under our ownership, we deploy significant capital to rebuild the facility. This includes implementing major component replacements, advanced tracker alignments, and inverter retrofits to establish new, state-of-the-art baselines.

Long-Term Principal Operations

The rebuilt asset enters our internal management system, where our team guarantees continuous operations, preventative maintenance, and long-term yield optimization for our institutional portfolio.
